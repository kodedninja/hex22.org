![Screenshot of my desktop](/content/wiki/workstation/desktop.png)

### Graphical

- Browser: [Firefox](https://mozilla.org/firefox) / [Beaker](https://beakerbrowser.com/)
- Terminal: [iTerm2](https://iterm2.com)
- Design: [Figma](https://figma.com)
- Window Manager: [Spectacle](https://www.spectacleapp.com/)
- Audio Workstation: [Ableton Live](https://ableton.com/live)
- Music: [Spotify](https://spotify.com/)
- Video Player: [IINA](https://lhc70000.github.io/iina/)
- Calculator: [Numi](https://numi.io/)
- Communication: [Telegram](https://telegram.org)

### Command-Line

- Text Editor: [kakoune](https://kakoune.org)
- Node: [n](https://github.com/tj/n) + [pnpm](https://github.com/pnpm/pnpm)
- Localtunnel: [localtunnel](https://github.com/localtunnel/localtunnel)
- Development Server: [budo](https://github.com/mattdesl/budo) / [http-server](https://npmjs.com/package/http-server) / [monote](https://github.com/stakit/monote)
- Image Processing: [korkut](https://github.com/oguzhaninan/korkut)
- Find: [fd](https://github.com/sharkdp/fd)
- Jumping Around: [z](https://github.com/rupa/z)
- ls: [exa](https://github.com/ogham/exa)

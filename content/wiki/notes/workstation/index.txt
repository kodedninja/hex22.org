text: Currently, I mainly use a 2020 Razer Blade Stealth with Windows (but using the Linux Subsystem 80% of the time), sometimes falling back to my 2015 Macbook Pro 13". The list below is a mixture of programs I use on both machines.

### Graphical

- Browser: [Firefox](https://mozilla.org/firefox)
- Terminal: [iTerm2](https://iterm2.com) / [Windows Terminal](https://aka.ms/terminal)
- Design: [Figma](https://figma.com)
- Window Manager: [Rectangle](https://rectangleapp.com/)
- Audio Workstation: [Ableton Live](https://ableton.com/live)
- Music: [Spotify](https://spotify.com/) / [cmus](https://cmus.github.io/)
- Communication: [Telegram](https://telegram.org)
- Todo List: [Microsoft To Do](https://todo.microsoft.com/)

### Command-Line

- Text Editor: [kakoune](https://kakoune.org)
- Node: [n](https://github.com/tj/n) + [pnpm](https://github.com/pnpm/pnpm)
- Dev Server: [budo](https://github.com/mattdesl/budo) / [http-server](https://npmjs.com/package/http-server) / [monote](https://github.com/stakit/monote)
- Find: [fd](https://github.com/sharkdp/fd)
- Jumping Around: [z](https://github.com/rupa/z)
- ls: [exa](https://github.com/ogham/exa)

I casually use:
- [pandoc](https://pandoc.org/)
- [localtunnel](https://github.com/localtunnel/localtunnel)
- [korkut](https://github.com/oguzhaninan/korkut)
- [you-get](https://you-get.org/)
- [monolith](https://github.com/Y2Z/monolith)

Hunor Karamán (b. 1999). Living in Vienna. ____ is compost is humus. A single material that is the past that is the future. From where beauty arises and where it returns to. Every branch of a tree is a tree. And what a single leaf carries in itself: already an entire forest. Writings appear in Hungarian literary magazines and on their own.
